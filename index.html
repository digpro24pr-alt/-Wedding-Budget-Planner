❓ How To Use
Everything you need in a few steps
1

Set Your Details

Enter names, wedding date, currency. Pick a theme color and toggle dark mode with 🌙.

2

Set Your Budget

Click the "Total Budget" card or go to the Budget tab and enter your total.

3

Add Planner Items

Add every vendor and budget item with prices and status in the Planner tab.

4

Build Your Guest List

Track RSVPs and type a table name — Seating fills in automatically.

5

Track Gifts, Tasks & Spending

Log gifts, create to-do tasks (overdue ones highlight), and record payments.

6

Compare Venues & Plan the Day

Compare six venues, then build a day-of schedule (auto-sorts by time).

7

Back Up & Share

Use Export/Import to back up and share with your partner. Or click "Load Sample Data" to explore.
